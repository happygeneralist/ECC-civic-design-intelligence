# In-school workshops

### Purpose
To understand how young people are feeling about their future, how they make decisions, and to understand what kind of, if any, online content might engage them.

### Research questions
What barriers do young people face when planning their future?
Who helps young people with decision-making? 
What makes online content engaging for them?
Which sources of information do they prefer?


### Method summary
- **Approach:** Group workshops (20-60 mins, depending on levels of need)
- **Recruitment:** The college or SEND leads identified young people to join the groups.
- **Sample:** ca 35 young people across different groups.
- **Data collected:** video recordings, audio recordings, transcripts, observation notes  
- **Analysis approach:** Thematic coding  

### Key findings
1.	Young people do have clear aspirations for the future but are less sure of the routes to access them.
2.	Young people with SEND are often following family pathways into employment.
3.	Young people prefer online content that they can customise, save and interact with.
